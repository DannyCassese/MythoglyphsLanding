A collection of 613 unique Mytho Glyphs created by Danny Cassese. Mytho Glyphs are an attempt to unite the most ancient and contemporary forms of art, creating the equivalent of cave paintings on the blockchain. Each Mytho Glyph has a deeper meaning beyond the surface, unique to each viewer. The symbols can be digested and understood in different ways by different people. Spend time with your Mytho Glyph and see what you can learn about the image, and maybe what you can learn about yourself! Mytho Glyphs are a part of Danny Cassese's larger career in art and are his first exploration into generative art on the blockchain. Thank you for taking the time to check the project out!

The mint is currently public at a price of 0.02 Eth.
