About

My name is Danny Cassese and I'm a painter and artist. I started painting in 2019 after being introduced to Basquiat. Prior, I had never painted or considered art as a life path. I have been exploring art and painting as I grow and try to consistently branch out to different mediums. I was introduced to NFT's after hearing about Beeple's success and began to research. I wanted to develop my own collection playing with the idea of generative art as a way to promote conversation and reflection surrounding art. Looking at the feelings and meanings art evokes from the viewer as opposed to the feelings and meanings of the artist. As I've developed this project I've learned so much and continue to learn and grow every day.
